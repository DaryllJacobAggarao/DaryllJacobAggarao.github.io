About Me

I'm a Police Officer.

I’m Police Officer Daryll Jacob B. Aggarao, proudly serving my hometown, Ilagan City, Isabela. As a law enforcer, I believe my duty goes beyond upholding the law. It’s about building trust, being fair, and showing compassion to those who need it most. Every day, I aim to serve with integrity, courage, and heart—because true service means putting others first. My goal is simple: to keep Ilagan safe and to be the kind of officer people can depend on. Justice, discipline, and respect guide me in every step, because serving my hometown is not just a job —it’s an honor.
